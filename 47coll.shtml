Collaborators

In the area of nonlinear waves, I currently collaborate with Hadi Susanto. In the past, I have collaborated with Linda Cummings (now at NJIT), and Alan Common (Kent). During my PhD work at Heriot-Watt University, I worked with Chris Eilbeck, Dugald Duncan, Chris Walshaw, Henrik Feddersen. Also during my PhD I collaborated with with Boris Malomed (Tel Aviv) and Nikos Flytzanis (Crete).

Work on nucleation and coagulation problems has involved Richard Graham, Peter Coveney, John King, Oliver Penrose, Jack Carr, Denver Hall, Fernando da Costa, Michael Grinfeld, Henry van Roessel, Raphael Sassportes, Zineb Mimouni.
